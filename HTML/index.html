A Big Mistery

The Bermuda Triangle, also known as the Devil's Triangle,
is an urban legend focused on a loosely defined region in the western part of the North Atlantic Ocean
where a number of aircraft and ships are said to have disappeared under mysterious circumstances.
The idea of the area as uniquely prone to disappearances arose in the mid-20th century,
but most reputable sources dismiss the idea that there is any mystery.
